# Contributing to Nirvana-OS

Contributions are welcome, under one firm condition: you must agree to the
Nirvana-OS Contributor License Agreement (CLA). Read it in [CLA.md](CLA.md).

## What agreeing to the CLA means

By contributing, you agree that:

- **You assign all economic rights** in your contribution to the copyright
  holder, Luiz Gustavo Vieira Rodrigues. The project becomes the owner of those
  rights and may use, modify, relicense, distribute, and sell the contribution.
- **You receive no compensation, ever** — no payment, royalties, revenue share,
  or equity.
- **You get no ownership and no stake** — contributing does not make you an owner
  of the code you wrote, a partner, an employee, or a shareholder of the project
  or any related company.
- **You keep only the moral rights** that Brazilian law makes inalienable
  (essentially attribution). Contributors may be credited in the project records.

If those terms are not acceptable to you, please do not submit contributions.

## How to contribute

1. Open an issue first to discuss non-trivial changes.
2. Fork, make your change, and open a pull request.
3. Agree to the CLA by signing off your commits (`git commit -s`) or by stating
   in the pull request: "I have read and agree to the Nirvana-OS CLA v1.0."

Pull requests submitted without CLA agreement may be closed without merge.

## Also welcome

- **Bug reports and feature requests.** Open a GitHub issue.
- **Security reports.** Do not open a public issue; contact the maintainer
  privately first.
